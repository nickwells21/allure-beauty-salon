Our Story

More Than a Salon. A Home for Your Career.

Built by a Stylist, for Stylists

Allure Beauty Salon was founded with one belief at its core: talented stylists deserve to run their own business without giving up a cut of what they've earned. After years in the beauty industry, our founder saw how commission structures could hold skilled professionals back — and set out to create something different in Evansville.

At Allure, we've built more than a salon. We've built a space where professionals show up, do great work, and leave knowing the business they're growing is truly theirs. Our tenants aren't employees — they're entrepreneurs. And we're here to support that.

We believe in the power of stylists supporting stylists. Many of our tenants came to us by word of mouth — one stylist telling another, "you have to see this place." That community energy is something we protect. If you're ready to be part of it, we'd love to hear from you.

Our Mission

To give licensed beauty professionals in Evansville the space, autonomy, and support to build a thriving independent business — on their terms.

Independence Is the Point

The Industry Is Changing — We're Built for It

More stylists are leaving traditional employment to own their chair, build their brand, and control their schedule. At Allure, we've been designed around that shift from day one. Booth rental and salon suite rental in Evansville, IN isn't just our business model — it's our commitment to every professional who walks through our door.
